Welcome to Equation Rock Popper beta!
 
Instructions:
- try to reach a highscore by solving the equations moving through the screen
- to send your entered solution, press 'enter'
- you can also discard the entered solution by pressing 'backspace'
- if you send the wrong solution, or an equation reaches the bottom, you lose one life
- if you run out of lives, the game is over
- do divisions without remainder (e.g.: 2/5 = 0; 7/3 = 2)
- press 'esc' at any time to quit game
- press 'enter' to start game
